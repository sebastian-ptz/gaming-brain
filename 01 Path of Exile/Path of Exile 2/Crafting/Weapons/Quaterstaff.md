## **Crafting**
### Physical Damage

**Source:** [Max-Phys Quasterstaff (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oq74sRPDAK8)

> Goal: Craft a high-physical-DPS Quarterstaff using essence + omen + desecration manipulation, finish with quality + corruption for a 4th rune socket.

#### Prerequisite Base
- A Quarterstaff base with **3 rune sockets** already rolled (see [[#Finding a Base with 3 Rune Sockets]] below).
- Decent item level (high enough to roll T1/T2 physical mods – typically ilvl 82+).

#### Step-by-Step

1. **Initial roll: chase Physical Damage**
   - Start rolling (essences / regal / exalt) until you hit *Increased Physical Damage* at **T2** along with *Increased Level of all Melee Skills*.
   - This is your anchor; commit to the item once both are present.

2. **Force a prefix slam (Exalt)**
   - Activate the **Omen of Sinister Exaltation** (next Exalted Orb only adds a prefix).
   - Deactivate every other omen.
   - Use a **Perfect Exalted Orb**.
   - You are hoping for either **Hybrid Physical + Accuracy** or **Flat Physical Damage**.

3. **If you roll a junk mod (e.g. Lightning) – remove it**
   - Open the **Ritual tab**.
   - Activate **Omen of Sinister Crystallization** (corrupted essence will only remove a *prefix*).
   - Use a **Perfect Essence of Haste** to scrub the bad prefix and try again.

4. **Re-slam the prefix**
   - Re-activate **Omen of Sinister Exaltation**.
   - Slam another **Perfect Exalted Orb**.
   - Target: Hybrid Phys / Accuracy (locked in this step in the video).

5. **Force the third prefix via Desecration**
   - Activate **Omen of Sinister Necromancy** (next desecration mod is forced to a *prefix*).
   - Use a **Preserved Jawbone** to desecrate.
   - The mod will be added to the prefix pool, leaving the suffix open.

6. **Reveal the desecrated mod at the Well of Souls**
   - Drop the Quarterstaff in the **Well of Souls**.
   - Activate **Omen of Abyssal Echoes** for a better reveal.
   - Reveal → you want **Physical Damage** at a high tier.
   - If the tier is bad, **reroll** the reveal until you land on a usable Phys tier (T5 in the video).

7. **Quality the weapon BEFORE corrupting**
   - Use **Blacksmith's Whetstones** to push quality to **20%**.
   - Do not skip this – quality is multiplicative with physical damage.

8. **Optional: Slam the open suffix**
   - If you have one open affix and don't want to keep gambling, **Perfect Exalted Orb** straight into it.
   - In the video this landed *Legion's* (phys damage as extra) – acceptable.

9. **Insert your 3 Iron Runes**
   - Socket **3 × Iron Rune** into the 3 rune sockets (more flat physical).

10. **Corrupt for the 4th socket**
    - Confirm quality is exactly **20%** (the community claim: quality > 20% prevents corrupt-success on first Vaal — keep it at 20).
    - Activate **every Omen of Corruption** you own (these bias the corruption outcome toward extra sockets).
    - Use the **Vaal Infuser** at the **Temple of Corruption**.
    - Target outcome: **4th rune socket** appears → socket a 4th Iron Rune.
    - Result in the video: 4 sockets, 4-star damage tier, **>1,000 physical DPS**.

> ⚠️ Corruption is destructive. Only Vaal once the prefixes/suffixes are locked in and quality is 20%.

## Finding a Base with 3 Rune Sockets

You cannot deterministically add rune sockets to a white/normal base – sockets on a Quarterstaff roll randomly when the item drops (1–3, with 4 only achievable via corruption). The cheapest path is to **buy a clean base on the trade site**.

### Trade Site Search (pathofexile.com/trade2)

1. Go to **https://www.pathofexile.com/trade2/search/poe2/<League>**.
2. Set **Item Category** → `Weapon` → `Quarterstaff` (or the specific base you want, e.g. *Striking Quarterstaff*, *Expert Striking Quarterstaff*).
3. Set **Item Rarity** → `Normal` (white). White bases are cheapest and let you do the full craft from scratch.
4. In the **Miscellaneous** section, add the filter:
   - **Sockets** → Min `3`, Max `3` (or leave Max blank for "3 or more").
5. Add the filter:
   - **Item Level** → Min `82` (so T1 prefixes such as max-tier Physical Damage can roll). Use `81` if you only need T2.
6. *(Optional)* Add **Quality** → Min `1` if you want it pre-qualitied, but for a fresh craft this is irrelevant – you will quality at the end anyway.
7. Sort by **Price: Asc** and buy the cheapest one in your league.

### Tips

- White (Normal) is the correct rarity. Magic/Rare bases come with pre-existing mods that will be in your way.
- Don't pay extra for **% quality** on a white base — Whetstones are cheap.
- 3 sockets on a 2H martial weapon is reasonably common, so the price is usually low early in a league and climbs as crafters chase max-roll weapons.
- If you can only find a 2-socket base for cheap, you cannot upgrade it to 3 sockets without RNG corruption — buying 3-socket directly is almost always more efficient.
- For the final corruption step, you want a finished, 20%-quality, fully modded weapon — the same trade strategy applies if you want to skip crafting and buy a finished 3-socket weapon to corrupt yourself.
