## What Is the Endgame?

The **Atlas of Worlds** is PoE2's core endgame system — an infinite map that expands in all directions, unlocked after completing all three acts on **Cruel** difficulty. You access it from the **Ziggurat Refuge** via the **Map Device**.

Key building blocks:
- **Waystones** — currency required to open map nodes; higher tier = harder map & better rewards.
- **Map Device** — the portal located in your Hideout or Ziggurat Refuge.
- **Atlas Passive Points** — earned by cleansing **Corrupted Nexuses** and completing **Unique Maps**; spent on the Atlas passive tree to specialize your mapping experience.
- **Tablets** — slotted into **Lost Towers** on the Atlas to empower nearby maps with content (Breach, Delirium, Ritual, etc.).
- **Boss Fragments** — collected from **Citadels**, used to fight Pinnacle Bosses such as **The Arbiter of Ash**.

![[Sources/Images/Path of Exile 2/poe2-atlas-of-worlds.png]]

## How to Unlock the Atlas of Worlds

1. Complete Acts 1–3 on **Normal** difficulty.
2. Complete Acts 1–3 again on **Cruel** difficulty.
3. Return to the **Ziggurat Refuge** and interact with the **Map Device**.
4. The first map opens the Atlas; from there, mapping is the main gameplay loop.

## Core Loop & Progression Path

The guide recommends this priority order once you're in maps:

1. **Cleanse Corrupted Nexuses** → earn Atlas Passive Points to specialize your tree.
2. **Slot Tablets on Towers** → empower nearby maps with the league mechanics you want to farm.
3. **Collect Boss Fragments** from Citadels → progress toward Pinnacle Boss fights.
4. **Push Waystone Tier** → higher tier = better drops, more passive points, access to gated content (e.g., Tier 6+ for Deadly Map Bosses, Tier 11+ for Delirium bosses).

## Special Atlas Locations

| Location | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| **Realmgate** | Portal where Map Device "inserts" (Breachstone, Simulacrum, Audience with the King) send you to face a specific Pinnacle Boss. |
| **Reliquary Vault** | Uses **Reliquary Keys** to roll random **Foil Unique** items. |
| **Citadels** (Iron / Copper / Stone) | Each contains a powerful boss that drops a **Crisis Fragment**. Collect all three to access the Arbiter of Ash. |
| **The Burning Monolith** | The endgame's final fortress — entry to the **Pinnacle of Flame** quest and Arbiter of Ash fight. |

## Endgame Mechanics List

Below is every mechanic listed on the source guide, with what it is, how it works, what it rewards, and Atlas tree recommendations from the linked sub-guides.

### Breach
- **What it is:** Dimensional tears tied to the **Keepers of the Flame** questline. Hiveborn forces invade through a Breach Hand.
- **How it works:** Dual-bar timer (dark purple decays, light purple extends from kills). Activate the Hand → kill Hiveborn → stabilize → defeat elites to collapse the Breach.
- **Rewards:** Hiveblood, Wombgifts, **Breach Splinters** (300 = one **Breachstone** → Realmgate → **Xesht, We Who Are One**). Feeds the **Genesis Tree** crafting system. Breachlords: **It That Was Tul**, **Esh**.
- **Atlas tree priorities:**
	1. **Frantic Invasion** — +100% Vruun spawn rate (massive density).
	2. **Reality Wound** — +10 seconds to Breach collapse window (huge QoL).
	3. **Wombgift / Hiveblood Quantity** — +5% each (currency throughput).
	4. **Sole Purpose** — Ailith skill specialization.

![[Sources/Images/Path of Exile 2/poe2-breach.png]]

---
### Delirium
- **What it is:** Timed fog encounters tied to the **Strange Reflections** questline. Step through the **Delirium Mirror** to engulf the map.
- **How it works:** Stay inside the fog; stepping out starts a countdown. Hit **Fractured Mirror Shards** along the path:
	- **Blue** — standard pack
	- **Purple** — empowered pack with better loot
	- **Gold** — Delirium-exclusive boss
	- **Red** — teleport to **Loathsome Mire** arena
- **Deliriousness** stacks across chained maps for tougher fights / better loot.
- **Rewards:** **Liquid Emotions** (instill amulet notables via Decanter of Madness), **Simulacrum Splinters** (300 = Simulacrum, 15 waves → +2 Atlas points to Delirium tree), **Delirium Tablets**.
- **Bosses:** **Omniphobia, Fear Manifest** & **Kosis, the Revelation** (Tier 11+), **Tangmazu, the Raven Trickster** (questline).
- **Atlas tree priorities:**
	1. **Come on! Face me!** — +25% unique boss shard chance; killing uniques pauses the timer 10s (great for fragment farming).
	2. **You can't scare me anymore!** — unique monsters drop Potent Emotions.
	3. **Grand Mirror Chance** — extra shards / Grand Mirrors.
	4. **Recurring Nightmares** — better fog spread for multi-map chains.

![[Sources/Images/Path of Exile 2/poe2-delirium.png]]

---
### Expedition
- **What it is:** Help the **Kalguuran settlers** excavate Verisium artifacts. You get **5 explosive charges** that chain from the previous placement.
- **How it works:**
	- **Excavated Chest Markers** — flagpoles = chests of loot & artifacts.
	- **Runic Monster Markers** — rare monsters that drop **Logbooks** and high-tier loot.
	- **Remnants** — tombstones with modifiers applied to the next blast (good and bad).
	- **Strategy:** prioritize **Runic Monsters**; only chain through Remnants that buff you. Use **Shift+V** to preview before committing.
- **Logbook Expeditions:** sub-event with **20 charges** and a chance to encounter the Pinnacle Boss **Olroth**.
- **Rewards:** Logbooks, four artifact types (Order, Broken Circle, Black Scythe, Sun), **Exotic Coinage** (refreshes vendor stock).
- **Atlas tree priorities:**
	1. **Detailed Records** — more Logbooks + guaranteed 3 implicit mods (this is the big one).
	2. **Legendary Battles** — more rare monsters & Exotic Coinage.
	3. **Disturbed Rest** — +50% Runic Monster Markers (more loot density).

![[Sources/Images/Path of Exile 2/poe2-expedition.png]]

---
### Ritual
- **What it is:** Sacrificial altars unlocked via the **Legacy of the Maji** questline at **Caer Tarth**. Appears around the world and in maps via **Ritual Tablets**.
- **How it works:** Kill mobs inside the circle, activate the altar; defeated monsters **revive and carry over** between the 3–4 altars per map. Five variants exist (Contaminated, Infested, Tainted, Smothering, Sapping) with different hazards.
- **Rewards:** **Tributes** spent on **Favours** (Uniques + Omens). You can **defer** a Favour (10% cost, returns 6% cheaper) or **reroll** the offerings for 1,000 Tributes.
- **Rite of the Nameless:** spend enough Tributes for **An Audience With the King** → fight the **King in the Mists** → obtain **The Head of the King** → run a 5-map gauntlet (mobs carry over between maps) → fight **The Bodach** Pinnacle Boss.
- **Atlas tree priorities:**
	1. **Increased Tribute** — +5% Tribute is universally good.
	2. **Prayer and Pledge** — 10% of Tributes spent count toward Audience progress (rushes the King fight).
	3. **Traveller's Woe** — guarantees specific Ritual types (lets you target-farm).

![[Sources/Images/Path of Exile 2/poe2-ritual.png]]

---
### Abyss
- **What it is:** Added in patch 0.3 / **Rise of the Abyssal** / The Third Edict update.
- **How it works:** **Fissures** spawn packs and rare enemies; clearing them reveals an **Abyssal Pit** with **Abyssal Invaders**. Sealing the Pit drops an **Abyssal Armory** (high-tier loot). Some Pits open caves into the **Abyssal Depths** — an undead city with 4 boss-bearing **Ancient Spires**.
- **Well of Souls (Act 2):** offer Abyssal-infused gear (or Waystones) to reveal hidden modifiers added by **Gnawed Jawbones** — pick 1 of 3 mod choices.
- **Rewards:** **Essence of the Abyss**, **Lineage Support Gems**, **Omen of Abyssal Echoes**, **Ulaman's Gaze** rune, new Unique items from the 4 Depths bosses.

![[Sources/Images/Path of Exile 2/poe2-abyssal-depths.png]]

---
### Corrupted Nexus
- **What it is:** A nexus at the heart of a **corrupted biome** (clusters of corrupted maps on the Atlas).
- **How it works:** Spot faint broken towers with corrupted tentacles in the Atlas fog → clear corrupted maps inward → defeat all rare enemies in the Nexus map → one of three corruption bosses spawns → kill it to purify the area.
- **Warning:** failing the Nexus map locks you out of that specific Nexus (other regions remain accessible).
- **Rewards:** **Crystalline Shard of Knowledge** (Atlas Passive Points), surrounding maps become **Cleansed Maps**.
- **Tip:** run Waystones with **minimal harsh mods** (especially avoid −res) to reduce failure risk.

![[Sources/Images/Path of Exile 2/poe2-corrupted-nexus.png]]

---
### Cleansed Maps
- **What it is:** Purified versions of corrupted maps that appear **after** clearing the local Corrupted Nexus.
- **How it works:** New monster types, unique bosses, exclusive mod pool. Choice: clear all corrupted maps **before** the Nexus, or rush the Nexus and return for the cleansed versions.
- **Rewards:** **Fracturing Orbs** — fracture a modifier on rare gear so it becomes permanent. Top-tier crafting tool.

![[Sources/Images/Path of Exile 2/poe2-cleansed-map.png]]

### Unique Maps
Special Atlas nodes with hardcoded mechanics — **they cannot have additional Tablet content layered on top**, so plan farming accordingly.

| Unique Map | Specialty |
| --- | --- |
| **Untainted Paradise** | Massive XP gain, **no item drops**. |
| **Vaults of Kamasa** | Filled with high-quality vaults & chests. |
| **Phaaryl Megaliths** | Exclusive rune drops. |
| **Fractured Lake** | Exclusive amulet/ring base types. |
| **Silent Cave** | Upgraded essences from imprisoned essences. |
| **Viridian Wildwood** | Wisps guide you to omen altars. |
| **Castaway** | Gold-rewarded shipwreck; boss **Torrek of the Drowned Fleet**. |

Also note the **Wandering Trader** (Nameless Seer) inside "Moment of Zen" maps — sells random Uniques.

![[Sources/Images/Path of Exile 2/poe2-nameless-seer.png]]

### Deadly Map Bosses
- **What they are:** Tougher versions of Act 4 / Interlude bosses with their own loot pools (Uniques, **Lineage Support Gems**).
- **Access:** Maps marked with a special icon, **Tier 6+ Waystones required**. You must complete a side objective in nearby maps to unlock the boss map.

| Boss | Map | Unlock Objective |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Avelyne & Varloch** | Mansion in the Mists | Activate **Draiocht Hengestones** |
| **Zahmir** | Temporal Sandstorm | Activate **Temporal Tethers** |
| **Ytzara & Maztli** | Estazunti's Vault | Find an **Idol of Estazunti** |
| **Manoki** | Eye of the Storm | Activate a **Karui Beacon** |

![[Sources/Images/Path of Exile 2/poe2-mansion-in-the-mists.png]]

---
### Realmgate
- **What it is:** Portal location on the Atlas that opens to dedicated Pinnacle Boss realms when you slot a Map Device key.
- **Keys & Destinations:**
	- **Breachstone** → Twisted Domain → **Xesht**
	- **Simulacrum** → Delirium Portal → **Omniphobia / Kosis**
	- **An Audience with the King** → Crux of Nothingness → **The Bodach**
- Each key is single-use.

---
### The Burning Monolith / Citadels
- **What it is:** "Most difficult area in the Atlas, and in all of PoE2." Houses the final Pinnacle Boss encounter.
- **How to unlock:** Reveal it on the Atlas, then take the quest **The Pinnacle of Flame**.
- **Three Crisis Fragments required** (one per Citadel):
	- **Ancient Crisis Fragment** — Iron Citadel
	- **Faded Crisis Fragment** — Copper Citadel
	- **Weathered Crisis Fragment** — Stone Citadel
- Citadel bosses are **Uber Act Bosses**; kill their lieutenants first or the faction relocates. Fragments are also tradeable / currency-exchangeable.
- **Reward:** **The Arbiter of Ash** Pinnacle fight → Unique items + **Arbiter Tree** Atlas passive points.
- **Atlas tree priorities (Arbiter Tree):**
	1. **Ancient Relics** — +25% rarity from Arbiter drops.
	2. **Witness to History** — 20% chance for an extra Unique.
	3. **Modular Servant** — 10% chance for valuable Socket Currency.

![[Sources/Images/Path of Exile 2/poe2-burning-monolith.png]]

> [!note]
> The Burning Monolith was **reworked in the Precursor Fortress update** — verify specifics in-game.

### Atlas Masters
Endgame NPCs offering 12-skill mini trees that tailor map bonuses. You can allocate **up to 4 points** per master, **reallocate any time**, and swap which master is active.

| Master | How to Unlock |
| --- | --- |
| **Hilda's Hunting** | Visit Hilda's Campsite SW of your Atlas start; complete Map Boss **Great Beast** hunts. |
| **Jado's Spycraft** | Complete a **Deadly Map Boss**; progress by killing more for him. |
| **Doryani's Science** | Cleanse a **Corrupted Nexus**; further by cleansing more & beating **Immured Fury**. |

## Atlas Passive Tree — What to Skill First

The Atlas passive tree is **your main customization lever** for the endgame. Suggested allocation order based on the source guide and linked mechanic pages:

1. **Mapping QoL first (always)** — pick up nodes that give **more Waystones**, **Waystones drop at higher tiers**, and **more rare/magic monsters**. These compound everything else.
2. **Pick ONE primary mechanic** to specialize in (don't spread thin early):
	- **Breach** if you want fast clear + currency throughput → take **Reality Wound** + **Frantic Invasion** first.
	- **Delirium** if you want raw loot quantity → take **Come on! Face me!** first.
	- **Expedition** if you like puzzle-style currency farming → take **Detailed Records** first.
	- **Ritual** if you want targeted Uniques/Omens → take **Increased Tribute** + **Prayer and Pledge** first.
3. **Corrupted Nexus / Cleansed Maps nodes** — guarantees more passive points & Fracturing Orbs (snowballs your tree and your crafting).
4. **Citadel / Boss Fragment nodes** — once you're stable on Tier 10+ maps, invest here to push toward **Arbiter of Ash**.
5. **Atlas Master nodes** — slot in the master that pairs with your chosen mechanic (e.g. Doryani's Science when farming Corrupted Nexuses).

## Recommended Progression Priority

A condensed roadmap for someone fresh into endgame:

1. **Hit maps → run Tier 1–4 with minimal mods** to learn the Atlas layout.
2. **Drop Tablets on every Lost Tower** you can reach, even with weak Tablets — empowered maps massively out-reward bare ones.
3. **Cleanse your first Corrupted Nexus** ASAP for the Atlas passive points lump sum.
4. **Pick your favorite mechanic** and commit ~10 passive points into its branch.
5. **Push to Tier 6+** to unlock **Deadly Map Bosses** and start hoarding **Lineage Support Gems**.
6. **Farm Breach/Delirium Splinters** for your first **Realmgate** Pinnacle fight (Xesht is usually the most accessible).
7. **Hunt Citadels** for Crisis Fragments → **Arbiter of Ash**.
8. **Optionally chase Unique Maps** (Untainted Paradise for XP, Castaway for gold) when you need a specific resource.
